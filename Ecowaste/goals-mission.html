Our Direction

Goal

EcoSort AI connects smart detection with community action. We are here to reduce waste, reward better habits, and build a reliable data signal that makes recycling infrastructure smarter every month.

Core Goals
  • Cut landfill-bound waste by turning every scan into a verified recycling action.
  • Grow a trusted dataset that improves sorting accuracy and reduces contamination.
  • Reward users with real incentives that keep sustainability habits consistent.
  • Partner with local facilities to improve recovery rates and collection routes.
Mission Statement

Our mission is to make proper recycling effortless and rewarding. We use AI to guide individuals in real time, translate their actions into measurable impact, and share that insight with the partners who keep the ecosystem moving.
